Preamble

This is some simple batter for fish and chips. I choose blue grenadier as it's a good balance of quality and price.

Ingredients

  • Fish of your choice, I use blue grenadier
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 1/4 cup baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Peanut oil, or other suitable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the flour, milk, water, baking powder, and salt in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Coat the fish generously and fry in oiln until golden brown
  3. Serve with some chips, lemon and tartar sauce.
